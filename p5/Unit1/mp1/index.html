Brother Baker
Ricardo Rhodes was an ordinary individual who was visited by disguised extraordinary travelers from another world.
Ricardo patiently explained to these travelers the basics of modern human society.
Moved by Ricardo's kindness despite being trapped in what they understood to be a barbaric societial and cultural machine of cutthroat misery.
They decided to grant Ricardo a boon.
Asking what Ricardo desired they heard him say, "Bread is pretty good".
With advanced nanomachines they constructed a Bread Atomization Ray in Ricardo's eyes.
Now Ricardo Rhodes is known by his new moniker, Brother Baker, as a man who altruisticly distributes glutten free bread to those desiring food.
For a small period of time, the world is just ever slightly better.
